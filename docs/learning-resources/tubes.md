## Visual Mathematics (Math Gym)

1. **3Blue1Brown – Essence of Linear Algebra, Chapter 3: *Linear Transformations***  
   Concept: 2D/3D linear maps as grid/basis transformations.  
   Experiment idea: Implement a 2D grid, apply arbitrary \(2 \times 2\) matrices, render the deformed grid and basis vectors interactively.  
   Gym: **Math**

2. **3Blue1Brown – Essence of Linear Algebra, Chapter on *Eigenvectors and Eigenvalues***  
   Concept: Eigenvectors as invariant directions under a transformation.  
   Experiment idea: Animate repeated application of a matrix to many vectors, highlight those that only scale (eigenvectors), visualize scalar field of growth/decay.  
   Gym: **Math**

3. **3Blue1Brown – *Gradient Descent, How Neural Networks Learn***  
   Concept: Gradient descent on a loss surface.

   Concept: Convolution of probability distributions as geometric overlap. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IaSGqQa5O-M)

   Gym: **Math**

   Concept: Geometric view of simple neural nets and decision boundaries. [youtube](https://www.youtube.com/c/3blue1brown)
